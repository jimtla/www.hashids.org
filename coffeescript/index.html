hashids

Available for JavaScript CoffeeScript Node.js PHP Go Ruby .NET C++ coming

  • hashids - Generate hashes from numbers, like YouTube or Bitly. Use hashids when you do not want to expose your database ids to the user.
  • Turn numbers like these: 3, 5, 7 into hashes like these: U6dc, u87U and HMou.
  • Use them in URL shortening, tracking stuff, or making pages private.
